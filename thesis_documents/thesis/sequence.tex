Essentially the first step of \algor{LCS} performs this
recursion:
\begin{eqnarray}
S_{0,j} &=& 0\\
S_{i,0} &=& 0\\
S_{i,j} &=& \max
\begin{cases}
  S_{i,j-1} & \\
  S_{i-1,j} & \\
  S_{i-1,j-1} + 1 & \textrm{if } A_i = B_j
\end{cases}
\end{eqnarray}
